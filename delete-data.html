Data Deletion Request – NexaChain Merchant

NexaChain Merchant is a non-custodial digital payment application. We do not store or control user funds, private keys, or recovery phrases.

What Data May Be Collected

  • Basic technical data (e.g. device information, app diagnostics)
  • Push notification tokens (if enabled)
  • Transaction metadata required for app functionality

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of any data associated with your use of NexaChain Merchant, please send an email to:

Email: Bioprotocol@proton.me

Include the following information:

  • App name: NexaChain Merchant
  • Your wallet address (if applicable)
  • Device model and operating system
  • A brief request for data deletion

What Will Be Deleted

  • Any stored technical or diagnostic data
  • Push notification identifiers

What Cannot Be Deleted

  • Blockchain transactions (public and immutable by design)

All valid deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe, subject to applicable legal and regulatory requirements.
